Project Summary

S~TV is a Melbourne/ Naarm based music platform documenting live performances and artist conversations in an intimate studio setting. Drawing influence from iconic music broadcast culture, it pairs performance with personality filmed live, minimal, and unfiltered, preserving the spontaneity and raw energy of the room.

Biography

Zac Main is a Melbourne filmmaker and currently studying at victorian university a bachelor of screen and media. His work has mainly focused on music related productions including music videos and live performance recordings. Over the past few years I has worked with many local bands helping create visual projects that capture the feeling and energy of live music. Alongside filmmaking I'm also involved in community radio hosting the program Self~Titled on 99.3FM Sunbury Radio. Through this he has interviewed musicians and built strong connections within the Melbourne independent music scene. This experience has helped shape the idea behind Self~Titled TV.

Professional Role

Role: Director, Editor, Host

I acted as Director, Editor, and Host on Self~Titled TV. My role involved developing the concept, coordinating and directing live studio performances and interviews, and overseeing the overall visual style of the series. I was responsible for filming decisions, pacing, and editing to ensure the final product reflected the raw, intimate, and performance-driven nature of the project.
